Retraction Optimizer

Test Pattern Generator to optimize retraction length and speed

How it works

Configure your printer and filament settings and generate a test pattern to print. Choose the setting with the least amount of stringing and also the least amount of gaps within the extrusions.

Use [hotend_temperature] and [bed_temperature] placeholders in your start G-code to automatically insert the configured temperatures.
